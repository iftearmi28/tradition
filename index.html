We look forward to welcoming you to Oitizzo soon. There are few easy steps to orders or reservations at Oitizzo. Follow the steps one by one, then you will successfully make an order or reservation. Here we go...

  1. Take a look of whole page and find out Order/reservation button.Then Click it. It will take you to our menu page.

  2. At menu page you will find our food items which are available on times. At every food item you will find cart button. Then find out a Order/reservation button which is located at the corner of navigation bar or top right corner. Click the Order/reservation button.

  3. After clicking Order/reservation button the page open up an order form. The form have 2 section one for order section another for reservation section.
    If you selected your items from menu items section then you will see those items in order form section with total amount. You also see 3 more input field. Filled out those input fields correctly. Then click next button.
    If you are interested in booking table then scroll down, you will find a Reservation section. There were 3 more input field. Filled out those input fields correctly. Then click next button.
    After clicking next button you will see a checkout form.

  4. Filled out all the checkout form field correctly and click submit button. You will receive a confirmation email once your order or reservation has been successful. If you do not receive this confirmation email, there may have been an error in your order or reservation.

    5. Note : Please view our reservation and cancellation policy ahead of your visit to Oitizzo.

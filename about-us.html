OUR STORY


OYTIJJO is one of the country’s most celebrated restaurants; the creation of leading chittagonian restaurant group, Fink, and Executive Chef. The restaurant is an organic space reflective of Chittagonian nature and traditional inspired cuisine. The interplay of textures and colour brings life and a vibrance that embraces the restaurant’s place in the dress circle of Chittagong Harbour. An ode to the Chittagong landscape, from the vast ocean floor, to the cracked bark of a paperbark tree, every detail from the ground up has been thoughtfully considered

Mezbani Launch and Dinner

Chattgayya represents their culture all over the country through some particular act.The "Chatgayya Mezban" is a felicitous example of hospitality. The word Mezban refers to a traditional party for hundreds of people who gather to join parties to eat traditional feast. This traditional feast is made with bangla recipes such as rice, beef with broth, Dals, etc. Like chittagonian tradition, Customer can have Mezbani launch and Dinner. We serve our customer this traditional meal to their plate. As a result, It has been the heighest sold item for the last 3 years.
